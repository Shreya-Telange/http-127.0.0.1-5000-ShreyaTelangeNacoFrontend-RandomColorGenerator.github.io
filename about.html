About Color Models Explorer

We aim to simplify complex color theories like RGB, CMY, and Web-Safe Colors through interactive tools and visuals—making learning fun and practical. This project was crafted by a developer passionate about colors, learning, and web interactivity—turning a simple idea into an educational tool for all. Whether you're a student, a designer, or simply curious—start exploring color models and level up your visual understanding today.
